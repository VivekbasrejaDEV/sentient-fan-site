What is Sentient?

Sentient is pioneering the next generation of Web3 infrastructure by creating an open, decentralized platform for artificial intelligence. By leveraging blockchain technology and distributed computing, Sentient enables AI models to be trained, deployed, and accessed in a trustless, permissionless manner.


The project aims to democratize AI by removing centralized gatekeepers and creating a global, open marketplace for AI services where developers, researchers, and users can collaborate without barriers.
